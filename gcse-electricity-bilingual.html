⚡
Electric Current
电流
diàn liú
▼
A flow of electrical charge. The size of the current is the rate of flow of charge. For charge to flow, a circuit must be closed and have a source of potential difference. In a single closed loop, current has the same value at every point.
电荷的流动。电流的大小是电荷流动的速率。要使电荷流动，电路必须是闭合的，并且有一个电位差来源。在单一闭合回路中，电流在每一点的值相同。
★ Q = It · Unit: ampere (A) 安培 · Symbol: I
🔋
Potential Difference (Voltage)
电位差（电压）
diàn wèi chā
▼
The energy transferred per unit charge between two points in a circuit. Also called voltage. It is what drives current around a circuit. Measured in volts (V) using a voltmeter connected in parallel.
电路中两点之间每单位电荷转移的能量。也叫电压。它驱动电流在电路中流动。用伏特（V）测量，使用并联连接的电压表。
★ Unit: volt (V) 伏特 · Symbol: V · Measured with a voltmeter in parallel
🚧
Resistance
电阻
diàn zǔ
▼
How much a component opposes the flow of current. The greater the resistance, the smaller the current for a given potential difference. Calculated using V = IR. Measured in ohms (Ω) using an ammeter and voltmeter.
元件阻碍电流流动的程度。电阻越大，对于给定的电位差，电流越小。用 V = IR 计算。用欧姆（Ω）测量，使用电流表和电压表。
★ V = IR · Unit: ohm (Ω) 欧姆 · Higher R = smaller current for same V
📐
Ohmic Conductor
欧姆导体
ōu mǔ dǎo tǐ
▼
A component where the current is directly proportional to the potential difference (at constant temperature). This means resistance stays constant. The I-V graph is a straight line through the origin. Example: a resistor at constant temperature.
电流与电位差成正比（在恒温下）的元件。这意味着电阻保持不变。I-V图是一条过原点的直线。例子：恒温下的电阻器。
★ I-V graph: straight line through origin 过原点的直线 = ohmic 欧姆的
💡
Filament Lamp
灯丝灯泡
dēng sī dēng pào
▼
A non-ohmic component. As current increases, the filament heats up, which increases its resistance. The I-V graph is a curved line — the gradient decreases as voltage increases. Energy is mostly wasted as heat and light.
非欧姆元件。随着电流增大，灯丝温度升高，从而增大其电阻。I-V图是一条曲线——随电压增大，斜率减小。大部分能量以热和光的形式散失。
★ I-V graph: S-shaped curve 曲线 · R increases as temperature increases
➡️
Diode
二极管
èr jí guǎn
▼
Allows current to flow in one direction only. Has very high resistance in the reverse direction. The I-V graph shows current only flows in one direction (forward bias). Used to convert AC to DC (rectification).
只允许电流在一个方向流动。在反向方向有非常高的电阻。I-V图显示电流只在一个方向流动（正向偏置）。用于将交流电转换为直流电（整流）。
★ One-way only 单向通行 · Very high R in reverse direction 反向电阻极大
🌡️
Thermistor
热敏电阻
rè mǐn diàn zǔ
▼
A component whose resistance decreases as temperature increases. Used in temperature-sensing circuits, e.g. thermostats. As the temperature rises, resistance falls, current increases. Useful application: automatically switching on a heater when it gets cold.
电阻随温度升高而减小的元件。用于温度传感电路，如温控器。温度升高时，电阻减小，电流增大。有用应用：当天气变冷时自动开启加热器。
★ Temperature ↑ → Resistance ↓ · Used in thermostats 温控器
☀️
LDR (Light-Dependent Resistor)
光敏电阻
guāng mǐn diàn zǔ
▼
A component whose resistance decreases as light intensity increases. In bright light: low resistance, high current. In darkness: high resistance, low current. Application: switching street lights on when it gets dark.
电阻随光照强度增大而减小的元件。在强光下：低电阻，高电流。在黑暗中：高电阻，低电流。应用：天黑时自动开启路灯。
★ Light intensity ↑ → Resistance ↓ · Used in automatic lighting 自动照明
🏠
Mains Electricity & Wiring
市电与接线
shì diàn yǔ jiē xiàn
▼
UK mains: 230 V AC at 50 Hz. Three-core cable: live (brown) — carries the alternating pd; neutral (blue) — completes the circuit at ~0 V; earth (green/yellow) — safety wire, only carries current in a fault. The live wire is dangerous even when a switch is open.
英国市电：230 V交流电，50 Hz。三芯电缆：火线（棕色）——携带交变电位差；零线（蓝色）——约0 V完成电路；地线（绿/黄色）——安全线，只在故障时导电。火线即使开关断开也是危险的。
★ Live=brown 棕 · Neutral=blue 蓝 · Earth=green/yellow 绿/黄
〰️
AC vs DC
交流电与直流电
jiāo liú diàn yǔ zhí liú diàn
▼
Direct current (DC): flows in one direction only. Produced by batteries. Alternating current (AC): the direction of current reverses repeatedly. UK mains is AC at 50 Hz (reverses 50 times per second). AC is used for mains supply because it is easier to transmit over long distances.
直流电（DC）：只在一个方向流动。由电池产生。交流电（AC）：电流方向反复改变。英国市电是50 Hz的交流电（每秒换向50次）。交流电用于市电供应，因为更容易长距离传输。
★ DC: batteries 电池 · AC: mains 市电, 50 Hz in UK
⚡
Power
电功率
diàn gōng lǜ
▼
The rate of energy transfer. Two equations: P = VI and P = I²R. Both must be recalled. Power is measured in watts (W). A 60 W bulb transfers 60 joules of energy every second.
能量转移的速率。两个公式：P = VI 和 P = I²R。两个都必须记忆。功率用瓦特（W）测量。60 W灯泡每秒转移60焦耳能量。
★ P = VI and P = I²R · Both must be recalled 两个都必须记忆
🔋
Energy Transfers
能量转移
néng liàng zhuǎn yí
▼
The energy transferred by an appliance depends on its power and how long it is on. Two equations: E = Pt (energy = power × time) and E = QV (energy = charge × voltage). Both must be recalled.
电器转移的能量取决于其功率和使用时间。两个公式：E = Pt（能量=功率×时间）和E = QV（能量=电荷×电压）。两个都必须记忆。
★ E = Pt and E = QV · Both must be recalled 两个都必须记忆

SERIES CIRCUIT RULES 串联电路规则
• Current is the same through every component: I is the same everywhere · 电流在每个元件上相同
• Voltage (pd) is shared: V = V1 + V2 + V3 · 电压（电位差）被分配
• Total resistance adds up: Rtotal = R1 + R2 + R3 · 总电阻相加
★ Adding more resistors in series INCREASES total resistance 串联更多电阻会增大总电阻
PARALLEL CIRCUIT RULES 并联电路规则
• Voltage is the same across each branch: V is the same everywhere · 每个支路电压相同
• Current splits: Itotal = I1 + I2 + I3 · 电流分流
• Total resistance is less than the smallest individual resistor · 总电阻小于最小的单个电阻（无需计算公式）
★ Adding more resistors in parallel DECREASES total resistance 并联更多电阻会减小总电阻
Equations
公式 — AQA 4.2 has the most equations of any topic · 本章节公式最多
MUST RECALL = memorise it. GIVEN = provided on equation sheet in the exam. HT = Higher Tier only.
Charge Flow 电荷流动 MUST RECALL
AQA 4.2.1.2
Q = I × t
Q = charge flow 电荷量 (C, coulombs 库仑)
I = current 电流 (A, amperes 安培)
t = time 时间 (s, seconds 秒)
WORKED EXAMPLE 解题示例
A current of 3 A flows for 20 s. Calculate the charge flow.
Q = I × t = 3 × 20 = 60 C
3 A的电流流动20秒。计算电荷流量。Q = 3 × 20 = 60 C
Ohm's Law / Resistance 欧姆定律 MUST RECALL
AQA 4.2.1.3
V = I × R
V = potential difference 电位差 (V, volts 伏特)
I = current 电流 (A)
R = resistance 电阻 (Ω, ohms 欧姆)
WORKED EXAMPLE 解题示例
A resistor has resistance 15 Ω. Current through it is 2 A. Calculate the pd.
V = I × R = 2 × 15 = 30 V
一个电阻器的阻值为15 Ω，通过的电流为2 A。计算电位差。V = 2 × 15 = 30 V
Resistors in Series 串联电阻 MUST RECALL
AQA 4.2.2
Rtotal = R1 + R2
Rtotal = total resistance 总电阻 (Ω)
Note: students are NOT required to calculate total resistance for parallel resistors. 注：不要求计算并联电阻的总阻值。
Power 电功率 MUST RECALL
AQA 4.2.4.1
P = V × I     P = I² × R
P = power 功率 (W, watts 瓦特)
V = potential difference 电位差 (V)
I = current 电流 (A)
R = resistance 电阻 (Ω)
WORKED EXAMPLE 解题示例
A kettle runs at 230 V with a current of 9 A. Calculate power.
P = V × I = 230 × 9 = 2070 W
Or using P = I²R: if R = 25.6 Ω → P = 9² × 25.6 = 2073 W ✓
一个水壶在230 V下以9 A运行。计算功率。P = 230 × 9 = 2070 W
Energy (from Power) 能量（由功率） MUST RECALL
AQA 4.2.4.2
E = P × t
E = energy transferred 转移能量 (J, joules 焦耳)
P = power 功率 (W)
t = time 时间 (s)
WORKED EXAMPLE 解题示例
A 100 W lamp runs for 30 s. How much energy is transferred?
E = P × t = 100 × 30 = 3000 J
一个100 W的灯泡运行30秒。转移多少能量？E = 100 × 30 = 3000 J
Energy (from Charge) 能量（由电荷） MUST RECALL
AQA 4.2.4.2
E = Q × V
E = energy transferred 转移能量 (J)
Q = charge 电荷量 (C)
V = potential difference 电位差 (V)
WORKED EXAMPLE 解题示例
A charge of 50 C passes through a pd of 12 V. Calculate energy transferred.
E = Q × V = 50 × 12 = 600 J
50 C的电荷通过12 V的电位差。计算转移的能量。E = 50 × 12 = 600 J
